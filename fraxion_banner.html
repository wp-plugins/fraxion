Your Fraxion Payments account will have 'Fraxions' stored in it. They cost around 1 US cent each and can be used at any web site that supports the Fraxion Payments system.
A Fraxion Payments account can be connected to many web sites and allows you to allocate your fraxions to unlocking articles at each web site. You may connect your login at this site to an existing account or make a new one.
